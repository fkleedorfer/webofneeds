buddyop back to ToC or Object Property ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/won/ext/buddy#buddy

Links a buddy Atom to one of its buddies. This should generally imply natural persons on both ends. We interpret buddies are people who like to do things together; such as engaging in sports or other social activities.

BuddySocketni back to ToC or Named Individual ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/won/ext/buddy#BuddySocket

Compatible with other BuddySockets, derives the buddy relationship. Installs local authorizations that <ol> <li> Gives buddies access to atoms on the https://w3id.org/won/ext/hold#HolderSocket by granting a token with scope https://w3id.org/won/ext/hold#ScopeReadHeldAtoms. </li> <li> Gives buddies access to the connections to other buddies using https://w3id.org/won/ext/buddy#ScopeReadConnectToBuddyOfBuddy </li> </ol>
