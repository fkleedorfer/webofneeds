Abstract

This ontology contains the definition of the *InterestSocket* the *InterestOfSocket* the *ExpertiseSocket* and the *ExpertiseOfSocket*, the *interest* the *interestOf* the *expertise* and the *expertOf* properties. These express control relationships in WoN. The Atoms with expertise/interest in other atoms represent agents (users or programs) and can themselves not be interests/expertises of other atoms.
